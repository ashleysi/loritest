My Services

Looking for a one-of-a-kind live music experience? Contact me with your ideas, and let's create a custom package tailored to your event. Whether it's for a group of students, dinner, or private party, I'll work with you to craft the perfect musical atmosphere that aligns with your vision. Let's bring your event to life with personalized and unforgettable live music.

Miss. Lori

Miss. Lori Music using songs, stories, scarves, drums, shakers, rhythm sticks, beanbags, teddy bears, beach balls, parachutes and more to make music FUN and entertaining every week!

1) “Care-giver and me” library classes- 45 minutes of fun movement and songs that engage babies, toddlers and adults.

Live Performances
