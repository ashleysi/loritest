My Services

Experience the magic of live music like never before!

Lori Hubbard brings electrifying energy to any event, creating unforgettable moments for your audience. Singer, song-writer and guitar player offering a wide variety of shows and sets for restaurants, bars, coffee houses, events, fairs, seniors, sing alongs, school age children, preschoolers and babies.
From intimate gatherings to large scale performances, she specializes in delivering a soul-stirring performance tailored to your unique vibe. Elevate your event with her live music expertise - where every note resonates, and every heart is moved. Lori has a big repertoire and a large base of songs and can tailor performances for all types of audiences. For “grown up” shows she is often accompanied by a pedal steel player, guitar player and/or a bassist. Kids shows are all Lori using songs, stories, finger plays and action songs.
